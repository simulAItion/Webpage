simulAItion is a concept born from the fusion of simulation, Artificial Intelligence, and innovation—a space where physics-based modelling meets AI-driven advancements to push the boundaries of engineering.

Originally launched as an aggregator of cutting-edge research and industry insights, simulAItion has grown into a recognised reference in the field, with thousands of professionals engaging with our content.

Our focus is on exploring and sharing how AI can enhance traditional simulation methods, with the long-term vision of contributing to the next generation of engineering tools, methodologies, and applications.

Our Mission

We aim to bridge the gap between traditional computational simulation and AI-powered techniques. As AI continues to transform engineering, simulation will play a fundamental role in the development of smarter, more efficient, and more autonomous systems. Whether in traditional CAE or AI-enhanced simulations, simulAItion is committed to advancing these technologies and making them more accessible.

Our Areas of Interest

Our core focus areas, backed by over a decade of experience, include:

  • Simulation Workflows – Complete simulation development across structural mechanics, particle-based methods, and multi-physics for advanced engineering applications.
  • Scientific Machine Learning (SciML) – Leveraging AI to enhance and optimise physics-based simulations.
  • Digital Twins & Robotics Simulation – Integrating AI and physics-based modelling for next-generation simulation-driven systems.

What We Do Today

Our current focus is on the following key activities:

  • Curating Knowledge – We continuously share the latest research, job opportunities, and events at the intersection of AI and simulation.
  • Learning & Experimentation – We explore and discuss emerging techniques, including AI-driven optimisation, digital twins, and AI-enhanced solvers.
  • Building Towards the Future – While we are not yet offering commercial services, we aspire to contribute through research, tutorials, and open discussions with industry experts.

The Future of simulAItion

Looking ahead, our long-term vision includes:

  • Training & Consulting – Helping professionals and businesses integrate AI-driven simulation.
  • Engineering & Research Collaboration – Exploring new methodologies in AI-powered simulation.
  • Simulation Software Development – Investigating AI-enhanced engineering tools.
  • Robotics & Digital Twins – Advancing intelligent simulation for next-gen automation.
